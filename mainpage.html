SWADESHI

Swadeshi showcases inventive Indian cuisine by complementing the flavours and traditions of India with global ingredients and techniques. Swadeshi, Jalandhar is ranked as the #22 Restaurant in Asia by Asia’s 50 Best Restaurants. It has been awarded the San Pellegrino ‘Best Restaurant in India’ by Asia’s 50 Best Restaurants for seven consecutive years (from 2015-2021).

The restaurant has also been rated as the ‘No.1 Restaurant in India’ by TripAdvisor consistently from 2014 – 2019. Recognized by Time Magazine ‘Amongst the World’s 100 Greatest Places’ and awarded the Number 1 Restaurant in India by Condé Nast Traveller.
